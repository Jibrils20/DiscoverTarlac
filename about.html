Our Missions

We believe that everyone should be able to travel, whether they have a week or a year, and whether they are on a backpacker budget or have money to spend. We feel that traveling is an effective means of spreading positivity around the world. We're passionate about providing methods that we can all travel more responsibly and be more aware of our effect.

What is a Travel Blog?

A travel blog is a combination of tools that allow travelers to keep a diary, transmit the address to family and friends, and set up automatic mailing lists so that friends receive an email every time you post a new entry to your list. Travel is also a motif, and the tools are built to accommodate your movement, with maps and flags linked from each diary. If you have a digital camera, you can upload photos to your blog. To assist future travelers, we urge our members to link to relevant information regarding specific places. Add images and write reviews, guides, and notebooks.
